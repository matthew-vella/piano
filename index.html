About

Matthew Vella is a student and pianist based in Malta. He started piano lessons at the age of 5 and is currently studying to attain his diploma in Music Theory and Piano Forte. In 2016 he won the Maltese Islands Festival Music Award for first place in Modern/Pop/Folk Piano.
